Common Questions

FREQUENTLY ASKED

Everything you need to know before starting your project with us.

Do you provide a free estimate?
Yes — all estimates are completely free of charge. We conduct a site visit, review your drawings, and prepare a detailed BOQ with item-wise rates. No obligation to proceed.
How long does it take to receive a quotation?
Typically within 24–48 hours of the site visit or after receiving your drawings. For larger projects, detailed BOQs may take 3–5 working days to ensure accuracy.
Do you work as a sub-contractor for main contractors?
Yes — this is our primary mode of operation. We are the preferred interior sub-contractor for many leading main contractors across Gujarat. We handle all interior trades under a single contract.
Which cities do you operate in?
We currently serve Ahmedabad, Gandhinagar, Mahesana, Vadodara, Rajkot, Pune, and Surat. For projects outside these areas, please contact us and we'll assess feasibility.
Do you handle both residential and commercial projects?
Absolutely. We work across residential homes, luxury apartments, commercial offices, retail showrooms, hotels, restaurants, hospitals, banks, and educational institutions.
What brands/materials do you use?
We use only approved, premium brands — Greenply, Centuryply, Hettich, Häfele, Saint-Gobain, Asian Paints, Philips, RR Kabel, and more. All materials are specified in the BOQ with brand, grade, and quantity.
Are there any hidden charges in your quotation?
Never. Our quotations are fully itemised with unit rates, quantities, and total costs. We believe in complete transparency — what you see in the BOQ is what you pay.

Joinery & Furniture Work

We deliver precision-crafted joinery and custom furniture solutions for commercial, retail, hospitality, and residential interiors. Our carpentry team works from approved drawings to produce built-in units, modular systems, and bespoke furniture pieces.

  • Custom modular kitchens & wardrobes
  • Commercial office joinery & workstations
  • Reception counters & display units
  • Veneer, laminate & painted finishes
  • Wall panelling & wainscoting
  • High-end hardware integration (Hettich, Blum)

Glass & Aluminium Work

  • Frameless & framed glass partitions
  • Aluminium window & door systems
  • Glass curtain wall & shopfronts

False Ceiling Works

We execute a wide range of false ceiling systems — from standard gypsum board to decorative wood, PVC, and metal grid systems. Each installation is precise, level, and coordinated with electrical, HVAC, and lighting requirements.

  • Gypsum board (single & double layer)
  • POP false ceiling & cornice work
  • Wooden coffered & slatted ceilings
  • PVC panel ceiling systems
  • Armstrong & metal grid tiles
  • Cove ceiling & indirect lighting recesses

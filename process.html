  Work Process

Our Work
Process

From First Site Visit to Final Handover — 10 Clear Steps

  Step by Step

How Every Project
Gets Done

A structured, transparent workflow that guarantees quality, documentation, and on-time delivery for every project we undertake.

01

Site Visit & Assessment

Our project manager visits the site with the main contractor to understand the full scope, existing conditions, and measurements. We document everything before preparing any estimate.

02

Estimation & Proposal

We prepare a detailed itemized estimate covering all trades, materials, quantities, unit rates, and timelines. Transparent pricing with no hidden costs.

03

Contract Negotiation

We review and negotiate terms with the main contractor, ensuring mutual clarity on scope, exclusions, timelines, and payment terms before any commitment.

04

Contract & LPO Signing

Formal sub-contract agreement and Local Purchase Order (LPO) are executed before any work begins. Complete documentation from day one.

05

Material Procurement

We source all approved materials from reliable vendors, with quality checks, proper documentation, and delivery schedules aligned to the project timeline.

06

Execution on Site

Our skilled tradesmen execute all works per approved drawings, specifications, and the main contractor's instructions. Daily progress updates are shared.

07

Quality Inspection

Multi-stage quality checks at each phase of work. Our site supervisors inspect every element before proceeding to the next stage — zero-defect policy.

08

Snag Clearance

After main execution, a comprehensive snag list is prepared and all items are resolved before calling for final inspection. Nothing is left pending.

09

Invoice Submission

Tax invoice submitted with complete supporting documents — delivery notes, work completion certificates, and GST breakdown for seamless processing.

10

Project Handover

Final walkthrough with the main contractor, documentation handover, and formal sign-off. Complete project archive handed to client.

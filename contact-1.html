FAQ

Common Questions

Do you work directly with end clients?

Primarily we work as a sub-contractor for main contractors, project managers, and architects. However, we do take on direct projects depending on scope and availability.

What areas do you serve?

We are based in Ahmedabad and primarily serve clients across Gujarat. We can also take on projects in neighbouring regions depending on scale and scope.

How soon can you start a project?

Start timelines depend on our current project schedule and your readiness. Once a BOQ is approved and work order is issued, we can typically mobilize within 7–14 working days.

Do you provide material or labour-only?

We offer both — material + labour packages or labour-only execution depending on the client's requirement and project structure.

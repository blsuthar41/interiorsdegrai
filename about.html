Our Journey

From Ahmedabad to Gujarat — Built on Execution

Degrai Interiors was established in 2020 in Amraiwadi, Ahmedabad with a clear mission: to be the most reliable interior execution subcontractor in Gujarat. We started with a small but highly skilled team, focusing on quality over quantity.

In just 5 years, we have grown to complete 55+ projects spanning corporate offices, luxury residences, hotels, retail showrooms and institutional spaces — always as a subcontractor partnering with main contractors.

Our strength lies in our multi-trade capability. Unlike single-trade subcontractors, Degrai Interiors can handle all 8 interior execution trades under one team, making us the preferred single point of contact for main contractors on complex projects.

Why Contractors Choose Degrai

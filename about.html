  Our Story

Who We Are

Degrai Interiors was founded in 2020 in Amraiwadi, Ahmedabad, with a clear mission: to be the most reliable interior execution partner for main contractors across Gujarat. From our first project, we set the standard for quality, documentation, and on-time delivery.

We specialize in interior execution as a sub-contractor, working closely with main contractors, architects, and project managers. Our role is to translate approved designs into flawlessly executed interior spaces — on schedule, within budget, and to the highest quality standards.

Today, with 55+ projects completed and 8 specialized trade capabilities, Degrai Interiors stands as a trusted name in the Gujarat interior execution industry.

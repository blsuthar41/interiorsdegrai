Who We Are

Interior Execution.
Done Right.

Degrai Interiors is an Ahmedabad-based interior execution company specializing in comprehensive fit-out services as a professional sub-contractor. Founded in 2020, we have grown into a trusted name for main contractors, project managers, and design firms across Gujarat.

Our core strength is execution — translating approved designs into reality with accuracy, craftsmanship, and consistent quality. We handle everything from structural metalwork and precision joinery to ambient lighting and decorative finishes.

With 55+ completed projects and a multi-disciplinary team of skilled tradespeople, Degrai Interiors is your reliable execution partner from site mobilization to final handover.

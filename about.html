Who We Are

THE DEGRAI STORY

Degrai Interiors is a specialized interior execution firm founded in 2020, headquartered in Amraiwadi, Ahmedabad, Gujarat. We operate primarily as a sub-contractor to main contractors on large-scale interior projects.

Our team of skilled craftsmen, site supervisors, and project managers brings deep expertise in all major interior trades — from false ceilings and joinery to glass work, stone cladding, electrical installations, and metal fabrication.

"To deliver flawless interior execution that transforms visions into reality — on time, within budget, and always exceeding quality expectations."

With 55+ projects successfully delivered across Gujarat, we have earned a reputation for quality, reliability, and timely project completion. Our work spans commercial offices, luxury residences, retail showrooms, hotels, restaurants, banks, educational institutions, and hospitals.

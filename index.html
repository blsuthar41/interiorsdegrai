About Degrai Interiors

Execution Excellence,
Built Since 2020

Degrai Interiors is a specialized interior execution company based in Ahmedabad, Gujarat. We work as a trusted sub-contractor for main contractors and project managers, delivering precise, high-quality interior work across commercial, retail, hospitality, and residential projects.

With a skilled team of craftsmen and a rigorous quality process, we transform design blueprints into flawlessly executed spaces — on time, within scope, and to the highest standards.

What We Do

Our Core Services

From precision joinery to ambient lighting — we execute every interior element with craftsmanship and care.

01

Joinery / Furniture Work

Custom woodwork, modular furniture, cabinetry, and built-in joinery crafted to precise specifications for commercial and residential interiors.

02

Glass & Aluminium Work

Partition systems, frameless glass, aluminium profiles, storefronts, and curtain walls designed and installed with precision.

03

False Ceiling

Gypsum, POP, wood, PVC, and metal false ceiling systems — designed for aesthetics, acoustics, and functionality across all project types.

04

Wall Paint

Professional wall finishing, texture coatings, specialty paints, and decorative applications for a flawless, durable result every time.

05

Upholstery & Curtains

Custom upholstery, fabric cladding, window treatments, and drapery systems that complement the interior design vision.

06

Stone & Corian

Natural and engineered stone cladding, Corian countertops, feature walls, and custom stone installations with expert craftsmanship.

07

Electric & Light Work

Interior electrical installations, LED lighting design, concealed wiring, smart lighting systems, and ambient lighting solutions.

08

Metal Work

Structural metal fabrication, decorative metalwork, SS railings, pergolas, laser-cut panels, and custom metal fixtures for interiors.

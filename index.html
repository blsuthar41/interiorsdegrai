About Us

Your Trusted Interior Execution Partner

Founded in 2020 and based in Amraiwadi, Ahmedabad — Arcmax Interiors operates as a dedicated interior execution sub-contractor, working alongside main contractors to bring design visions to life with precision, timeliness, and impeccable quality.

From Joinery and Glass work to False Ceilings, Wall Paint, Upholstery, Stone, Electrical and Metal work — we handle every trade under one roof.

What We Do

Our Core Services

Comprehensive interior execution services delivered by specialist tradespeople with precision and care.

Joinery & Furniture Work

Custom woodwork, fitted furniture, cabinetry and joinery crafted with precision for every space.

Glass & Aluminium Work

Partitions, facades, windows, doors and decorative glass elements with premium aluminium systems.

False Ceiling

Gypsum, POP, Wood and PVC false ceiling systems with clean lines and integrated lighting solutions.

Wall Paint

Decorative and textured wall finishes, premium emulsions and specialty paint applications.

Upholstery & Curtains

Soft furnishings, custom curtains, blinds and upholstered panels tailored to your design vision.

Stone & Corian

Natural stone cladding, marble flooring, Corian countertops and decorative stone surfaces.

Electric & Light Work

Complete electrical installations, smart lighting systems, cove lighting and feature lighting design.

Metal Work

Structural and decorative metalwork, stainless steel, MS fabrication and powder-coated finishes.

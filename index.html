What We Do

OUR SERVICE DISCIPLINES

Comprehensive interior execution across all trades. One trusted partner for every discipline — delivered with precision, on time, within budget.

Joinery & Furniture

Custom kitchens, wardrobes, workstations, reception counters, and bespoke furniture with premium hardware.

Glass & Aluminium

Frameless partitions, curtain walls, ACP cladding, aluminium doors, windows, and shopfront systems.

False Ceiling Works

Gypsum, POP, wooden, PVC, Armstrong, and cove ceiling systems coordinated with MEP services.

Wall Paint & Finishing

Emulsion, texture, Venetian plaster, stucco, and exterior weathercoat with Asian Paints & Kansai.

Stone & Corian Work

Marble, granite, sandstone, slate, and Corian solid surfaces — fabricated and installed with precision.

Electric & Lighting

Concealed wiring, DBs, LED strips, cove lighting, downlights, and smart dimmer systems.

Metal Fabrication

SS/MS railings, laser-cut screens, pergolas, canopies, custom metal furniture, and powder coatings.

Upholstery & Curtains

Custom drapes, roller blinds, motorised tracks, fabric panels, and sofa reupholstery.
